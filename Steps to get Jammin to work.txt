*Side Notes
*Assuming you've already created your React app within your command line (FIRST!)
*Add the corresponding files to your project

Step 1: Go to Spotify's Developer Website and Sign In
https://developer.spotify.com/dashboard/login

Step 2: Create an App with any name to obtain a client Id.

Step 3: Click Edit Settings

Step 4: Add a Redirect URI to: http://localhost:3000/ (once added save settings at the bottom)

Step 5: Open Spotify.js and change clientId (at the top) to your own personal ID (make sure its formatted like: 'PasteClientCODE').
